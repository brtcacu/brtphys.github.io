About Me

Fourth-year Physics undergraduate at Bogazici University pursuing a double major in Mathematics, with growing specialization in mathematical physics.

My current research interests focus on the intersection of theoretical physics and advanced mathematics, particularly in General Relativity and its modern extensions.

I am deeply engaged with foundational questions in Quantum Field Theory and alternative approaches to gravity, with special attention to entropic gravity frameworks. This dual physics-mathematics perspective informs my approach to theoretical problems, where I seek to combine physical intuition with mathematical precision, particularly in studying emergent spacetime phenomena.
